   Interpretation: how much hybrid breaks already good mesh areas.

3) selectivity_score = repair_gain - preserve_damage
   Interpretation: single scalar for "fix hard places without breaking good places".

4) leakage_good
   mean( alpha_eff ) on good_region, where alpha_eff = gs_alpha * gate
   Interpretation: how much GS contribution enters zones where mesh is already good.

   Metric = mean( max( |G(hybrid)-G(gt)| - |G(mesh)-G(gt)|, 0 ) )
   Interpretation: positive means hybrid introduces extra blur/edge loss vs mesh.

6) good_region_ratio, bad_region_ratio
   Interpretation: sanity that selected regions are non-empty and stable.
